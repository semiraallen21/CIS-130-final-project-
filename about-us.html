Our Mission is to create unforgettable experiences

We are a concierge-level event planning and design boutique. We plan, design, and execute your celebration using a custom event planning process for each individual client to create a seamless and love-filled occasion from start to finish.

Established in 2014, Bespoke Event Design was born from the experience of helping two close friends plan their interfaith, intercultural, international, wedding. We realized that one of the most beautiful aspects of our world is the breaking of barriers that these types of weddings and events create. We've moved beyond the old-school standard wedding format and our goal is to help amazingly unique couples, have dream weddings as unique and beautiful as their relationship.

We also strongly believe that the marriage should be what a couple puts the hard work in for and the wedding should just be the fun! That's why we take care of everything from event design to budget guidance, vendor coordination to etiquette advice, and everything in-between. And on your wedding day, we’ll discretely take the reigns so that you, your fiancé, and your families can enjoy your wedding day to the fullest.

We take on a limited number of weddings per year in order to maintain our high standard of customer service so don't wait to reach out!
